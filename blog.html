Atheva Services is a Top HR Services Firm for 2020

At Atheva, we are your outsourcing partner to find the best services. Built by a professional team that stands tall with a reliable multilingual and multifaceted recruitment and accounting experience, our team consists of technically advanced professionals whose experience and periodic training provided by the company keeps them updated with the latest recruitment possibilities

Clutch has just announced the top 50 human resources services firms as part of their Small Business Solidarity program. We are thrilled to announce that Clutch has named Atheva Services a top HR services company for 2020! You can find us listed among the top human resources companies on Clutch.

Located in the heart of Washington, DC, Clutch is a B2B ratings and reviews platform. Their team conducts independent research into B2B service providers. By directly interviewing the former customers of companies on their site, they ensure that all data on their site is verified. These reviews from clients form the basis of their rankings and awards.

“Human resources are a vital, yet often overlooked, element of an organization. From culture to benefits, it is important for an organization to do best by its employees,” said Clutch Business Development Analyst Dustin Sammons. “The right human resources consultant can help you continuously improve and take your organization to the next level.”

Clutch also has a sister site, The Manifest! The Manifest is another B2B resource. Users can look through shortlists of companies according to location and service line or peruse how-to guides and business survey data. We are honored to be listed among the top HR firms on the Manifest!

We are thankful for each and every one of our wonderful customers, especially those that took the time to leave us a review on Clutch! Hear what they had to say about us.

“They truly work to understand the way we work as a company and what we want. We speak the same language, per se. They don’t just say they can’t do something.” – Commercial & Operations Director, Medical Recruitment Firm

When our customers succeed, we succeed! This award and our perfect 5-star rating on Clutch are all thanks to you, our amazing clients!
